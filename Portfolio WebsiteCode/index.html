MY PROJECTS





WORK EXPERIENCE

INTERNSHIP AT HAUGUM PRODUCT DESIGN, TRONDHEIM (NORWAY)


In February 2016, I assisted a product designer called Ragnhild Haugum. At that time she was working with a family who were planning to transform their land property into a theme park. I had the opportunity to design a tower model, which I came up with by myself. Later, I had the opportunity to present the tower to the family and propose a recreation of that tower for their theme park. Other than that, the purpose was for them to gain inspiration for a tower or similar structures.

VOLUNTARY YEAR AT GOLDNETZ gGmbH, BERLIN (GERMANY)


In October 2018, I started a voluntary year at a company that helps and coaches people (mostly migrants) who want to enter or re-enter the working world. My work there consisted of assisting the coaches and performing clerical activities. During my stay at this firm, I created a calendar in Excel for managing my week and keeping hand of my appointments. The managing director of the firms office where I was employed, admired the calendar and introduced it to the office. This particular calendar was then introduced other departments for further use. On the right, a recreation of this calendar can be seen, which I use now to organize my studies.
